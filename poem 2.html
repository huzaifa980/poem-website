Featured Poem

On the open road

On the open road,
with wheels that spin,
A symphony of motion,
a vibrant din.

The engine roars,
a melody of power,
As we embark on this journey,
hour by hour

Through winding lanes and stretches wide,

The car becomes our vessel,
our guide.

With windows down,
wind tousling our hair,
We're adventurers,
free spirits without a care.

The asphalt beneath us,
a ribbon of dreams,
Unfolding horizons,
as far as it
The landscapes change,
like a vibrant art,
Mountains, valleys,
and shores that impart.
