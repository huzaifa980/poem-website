Featured Poem

In a land of dreams and endless fun,

In a land of dreams and endless fun,
Lived a kid, bright as the sun.

With laughter and friends, every day was a race,
Exploring, learning, a smile on their face.

They swung and slid in the playground's delight,
Imagination soared to incredible height.

Numbers and stories filled their young mind,
With dreams and wonders yet to find.

In the classroom, they chased their dreams,
With passion and curiosity, bursting at the seams.

They wrote, they learned, they aimed for the sky,
Their spirit shining, ready to fly.

So keep on learning, little one,
Embrace the world, bask in the sun.

You're brave, you're strong, and filled with bliss,
Unleash your greatness, and find pure bliss.
