Featured Poem

In a classroom filled with joy and cheer,

In a classroom filled with joy and cheer,
Class three kids, oh, how they appear!

Counting numbers, learning with delight,
Imaginations soaring, reaching new height.

Books and colors, their passions ignite,
Creating art, a beautiful sight.

Playing in the sun, friendships bloom,
Innocence and laughter, filling every room.

Class three kids, full of wonder and glee,
Chasing dreams, so wild and free.

Keep learning, exploring, never shy,
Class three kids, reach for the sky!
